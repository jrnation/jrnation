Privacy Policy

📅 Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to The Linux Hub. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may provide while using our centralized documentation and tutorials.

2. Information We Collect

Because The Linux Hub is a static site focused on providing open-source documentation, we do not require user accounts, and we do not actively collect personal data such as names or email addresses unless you voluntarily contact us.

3. Log Files and Analytics

Like most standard websites, we utilize log files and analytics services (such as Cloudflare Analytics) to understand how visitors use our site. This information includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, and the number of clicks. This data is not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

4. Google AdSense and DoubleClick Cookie

To keep this hub free for all users, we use Google AdSense to display advertisements.

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on our site.
  • Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of the use of the DoubleClick cookie for interest-based advertising by visiting the Google Ads Settings webpage.

5. Third-Party Links

Our guides frequently link to external open-source projects, GitHub repositories, and Linux distributions. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these third-party websites.

6. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its Terms and Conditions.
